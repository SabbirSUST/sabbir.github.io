About Me

I am Mohammed Sabbir, graduated from the department of Computer Science and Engineering, Shahjalal University of Science and Technology in 2013. I have around 10 years of industry experience in working with Application Development. I have worked for Leads Corporation, RITL, Woori Bank and Shopno Code Software Limited within last 10 years of my career..

Bio

After my graduation, I decided to work for LEADS Corporation Limited, the global leader in Banking Solution and Consulting services for the Bank and Other Industry. My tenure as a software engineer at Leads provided me with ample opportunity to work with a highly experienced engineers. I worked on .NET framework and SQL Server. I also worked on Data Center and Implementation.

Back in 2015, I moved to Woori Bank (Bangladesh), the largest Bank in South Korea. I joined their IT team as an IT specialist. Besides general desk job I developed several in-house application such as GOAML, Debit Card Management, CBI Management. As an IT Officer I also manage Woori Bank Data Center(Head Office), prepare and sent verious reports to Bangladesh Bank, prepare Audit report.

In 2018, I joined to Shopno Code Software Limited, As a Senior Software Engineer. Later on share holder of the company. Current worked as Head of IT. Here we have multiple applications such as POS, ERP and HRM solution.
